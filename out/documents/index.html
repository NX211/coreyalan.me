Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign a document?

Click on the document you need to sign, review its contents, and follow the on-screen instructions to add your signature.

Are my signatures legally binding?

Yes, all signatures are legally binding and compliant with e-signature regulations including ESIGN and UETA.

Can I download signed documents?

Yes, once a document is signed, you can download a copy for your records. You'll also receive an email with the signed document.

What if I need to make changes?

If you need to make changes to a document, please contact our support team. We'll help you with the necessary modifications.
